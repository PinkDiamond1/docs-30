xrp-ledger.toml Checker

If you run an XAG Ledger validator or use the XAG Ledger for your business, you can provide information about your usage of the XAG Ledger to the world in a machine-readable xrp-ledger.toml file.

This tool allows you to verify that your xrp-ledger.toml file is syntactically correct and deployed properly.
